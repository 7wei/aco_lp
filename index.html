FAQs

Is ACOHEALTH health insurance? 
ACOHEALTH is not health insurance. It is a Primary Care membership at ACOHEALTH Partner Clinics, meaning we contract directly with you to provide primary care services for a low annual fee, much like a gym membership
We do encourage our members to have at least a major medical insurance plan, and preferably a health savings account, if they are able. We do not have insurance agents on staff, but we are happy to refer you to one.
Is health insurance a requirement to become a ACOHEALTH member? 
Absolutely not! ACOHEALTH is a primary care membership. One of the main goals is to make primary care more accessible to people without health insurance.
As mentioned above, ACOHEALTH OneCare plans are not insurance. We recommend that members still carry at least some type of major medical plan or catastrophic insurance. We do not have insurance agents on staff, but we are happy to refer you to one.
Why pay an annual fee? What is in it for me as a patient? 
We pay for so many things via subscription for unlimited use: Internet and Mobile plans, Astro and Netflix. We believe healthcare should be the same.
Instead of unpredictable medical bills with no annual screening, our members pay an annual retainer, or membership fee, instead. The pricing is clear, no hidden fees, copays or coinsurance charges for services that are included in the membership.
You can become a member regardless of your current health condition, age, gender, citizenship or immigration status.
I am healthy and don’t go to the doctor often. How would ACOHEALTH benefit me? 
Our goal is to keep members as healthy as possible with improved access to affordable primary care. Our providers and care teams devote time to educate on prevention and lifestyle.
Most unexpected visits to the hospital emergency room that would have emptied your pocket can easily be covered in a same-day appointment. This will likely save you enough to justify the cost of one year of membership!
Are there any exclusions for pre-existing conditions? 
Absolutely not! There are no pre-existing condition exclusions and there are no increases in the membership fee based upon prior health history.
In fact, senior citizens and those with chronic medical conditions are often the members who receive the most significant benefit of our services.
What does the monthly ACOHEALTH membership fee cover? 
The ACOHEALTH membership fee covers access to primary, preventive, and urgent care services to help you manage ongoing health conditions. Some situations may trigger extra costs (such as labs and medications outside of those included and more complicated services.) The ACOHEALTH care team will help you navigate any expenses you might incur.
How does ACOHEALTH work with my current insurance or company health plan? 
As an ACOHEALTH member, we will not bill your insurance or your employer for any services covered by your membership. 
If your employer is keen on enroling their staff to a OneCare plan, do contact us for more information.
How are office visits handled? Do I need an appointment? 
It’s easy – schedule a visit via WhatsApp - and come into the designated clinic at your scheduled time.
While we offer the same or next day appointments to our members, we do need to schedule all appointments in advance. All you need to do is send us a whatsapp message to your dedicated Member Care Services officer to set up your next appointment.
If you need immediate urgent care, you can also visit one of our ACOHEALTH clinics without an appointment. Note that only two urgent care visit per calendar year are included in your ACOHEALTH membership.
What happens if I need external care? Specialists, diagnostic tests, emergency room, hospital? 
ACOHEALTH does not provide coverage outside of services provided in the membership and the services included in the membership are only ones performed with ACOHEALTH Medical Center providers.
Therefore we recommend that all ACOHEALTH members have at least a high-deductible insurance plan for unexpected and catastrophic events, such as surgery, emergency room visit, or hospitalisation.
Always try to talk to us first before you seek external care but call 999 or your local hospital in case of an emergency. Often, it will likely be something we can take care of saving you the time and money of having to go elsewhere.
If you’re admitted into the hospital, the hospital’s doctors will be the ones taking care of you in the hospital. If needed, our doctor will be happy to be a source of information to the hospital doctors and help coordinate your care in the hospital. And we will be ready to assist with your outpatient care when you get out.
Can ACOHEALTH work for children? Pediatrics? 
Absolutely! We are happy to accept children. We can provide all standard child health care services, including annual physical exams, sick child healthcare (sore throats), and chronic disease control asthma, allergies, etc. Periodic consultations are included and encouraged. Some medications may not be covered under the OneCare plan but are available at discounted rates.
Are vaccines provided? 
At this time, we only offer the seasonal flu shot and COVID vaccinations (based on availability). For medications and supplements not covered by the monthly fee, we offer significant discounts.
How and when can I sign up? 
You may sign up at any time by clicking here, whatsapp 018 3699 246. There is a one-time enrolment fee per person, which is waived during the pre-launch period.
When you enrol, you will be able to select your preferred clinic and date of appointment. A membership welcome email will also be sent to you.
What payment methods are accepted? 
You can pay online by bank transfer.
May I re-start my membership after canceling? 
We do allow for re-enrolment after cancellation, but you will be treated as a patient signing up for the first time. All enrolment paperwork must be completed, and you will be charged the enrolment fee again.
Can you provide memberships (virtual visits, etc.) to people "out of state"? 
If a member establishes care in person, we can provide problem monitoring over the phone or the Internet for some time. Some care (visits due to illness, etc.) must be provided in person, so we do not recommend people to join our plan unless they can visit us in person regularly.
Can I be seen without becoming a member? 
Yes, but you never know what the future holds for your health, so we always suggest joining for a full membership to take advantage of the comprehensive services and to get ahead of any problems that may pop up.
If you aren’t sure if a membership is right for you, whether it be financial, for your health, or otherwise, you can always schedule a consultation with us to help you work through your decision. You can whatsapp us at 018 3699 246.
How do employers benefit from enrolling their employees in ACOHEALTH? 
A healthy employee is a happy and more productive employee. ACOHEALTH membership ensures your employees (and their families) get preventive care and same day urgent care access, as well as discounted lab and medications. This decreases sick leave while also improving employee satisfaction, recruitment, and retention.
To learn more and get quoted for group rates, whatsapp us at 018 3699 246.
