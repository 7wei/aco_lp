Introducing Aco Health’s OneCare Plans

A membership program that gives you direct access to healthcare services at affordable prices.

Unlimited Visits. Clinic visits as often as you need.
Complimentary Health Screening. Once a year, so you know what’s going well & what needs to be improved on
High-quality Care. Each doctor takes the time to focus on your needs.
Personalised Care. Doctors you can trust. Set your health goals & they will help you to reach them.
Convenient Access. 3 locations in JB with more to come. Same/next-day appointments that start on time.
Available to everyone. Accepted regardless of your current health condition or age.

What you get with an Aco Health OneCare membership

You pay an affordable fee for the services included in your membership. If there is an extra cost for a service that is not included, you will know the cost before moving forward. Simple and transparent, as healthcare should be.
